<h2>2. Core Calculation Stages</h2>
<h3>Stage A — Ingestion Quality Control</h3>
<p>Each raw event is assigned a quality weight based on source reliability history, duplicate density, parsing completeness, and geo confidence. </p>
<h3>Stage B — Detector Scoring (15 Families)</h3>
<p>The detector layer transforms each normalized post into detector intensities:</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Distress family</strong>: food/water/health stress markers.</li>
<li><strong>Anger and escalation family</strong>: aggression, mobilization language, urgency verbs.</li>
<li><strong>Institutional legitimacy family</strong>: governance rejection and trust erosion signals.</li>
<li><strong>Identity polarization family</strong>: group-framing and exclusion language.</li>
<li><strong>Information warfare family</strong>: rumor, contradiction, synthetic amplification patterns.</li>
</ol>
<h3>Stage C — Temporal Smoothing and Burst Control</h3>
<p>To avoid overreaction to one-off spikes, each detector stream is smoothed with exponentially weighted moving averages and burst clamps.</p>
<h3>Stage D — PulseState Assembly</h3>
<p>PulseState is assembled from grouped detector vectors:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>ScarcityVector</strong> from distress, access, service-breakdown indicators.</li>
<li><strong>ActorStress</strong> from institutional conflict, mobilization, and pressure cues.</li>
<li><strong>BondStrength</strong> from trust and cohesion evidence (inverted for risk).</li>
</ul>
